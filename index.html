Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Beauty

Beniel's Beauty Training College & Salon was founded with a singular vision: to create a world-class beauty education experience right in the heart of Kisumu, Kenya. We believe that every woman deserves the opportunity to transform her passion into a sustainable, fulfilling career.

Located in Carwash, Kisumu, our institution has become the leading destination for aspiring beauty professionals across Western Kenya. Our industry-certified instructors bring real-world salon experience directly to the classroom.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll feel the difference — a warm, empowering environment designed to help you grow, create, and thrive.
